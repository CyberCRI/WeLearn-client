## **Privacy Policy – WeLearn**

### **1\. Who are we?**

WeLearn is a platform developed by the **Learning Planet Institute (LPI)**, a non-profit organization whose goal is to support teaching and learning, particularly on sustainability issues.

**Data Controller**  
Learning Planet Institute (LPI)

**Data Protection Officer (DPO)**  
Email: **lamis.chamas@learningplanetinstitute.org**

---

### **2\. Scope of this policy**

This privacy policy describes how your data is processed when you use the WeLearn platform, which can be accessed without creating a user account.

It covers the following features:

- resource search,
- chat,
- syllabus generation from documents provided by the user,
- feedback form.

---

### **3\. General principles**

The Learning Planet Institute places particular importance on personal data protection and applies the following principles:

- collection limited to strictly necessary data,
- no commercial purpose,
- no resale of data,
- transparency about the processing performed,
- data hosted within the European Union (mainly in France).

---

### **4\. Data processed by feature**

#### **4.1 Resource search**

When you perform a search:

- the entered terms are processed temporarily to produce a response,
- no data is stored,
- queries are not retained after the response.

---

#### **4.2 Chat**

When you use the chat:

- messages entered are processed temporarily to generate a response,
- chat content is neither stored nor reused,
- no AI model training is performed using these exchanges.

Please note:

- the platform does not aim to collect personal data through the chat,
- however, you are free to enter personal information in your messages if you choose.

---

#### **4.3 Syllabus generation from documents**

For syllabus generation:

- you can upload documents (PDF, text, etc.),
- these documents are processed solely to produce the requested response,
- files are not stored on the platform,
- they are temporarily transmitted to the necessary processing services for result generation and then deleted.

---

#### **4.4 Feedback form**

When you fill out the feedback form, the following data is collected:

- name,
- email address,
- profession.

These fields are required to:

- ensure that feedback comes from the intended audience (higher education),
- limit noise and irrelevant responses,
- enable qualitative follow-up of the feedback.

**Purpose of processing:**

- satisfaction measurement,
- product improvement,
- possible follow-up on feedback provided.

Responses to the form are accessible only to the internal WeLearn project teams.

---

### **5\. Legal basis for processing**

The processing described above is based on:

- the legitimate interest of the Learning Planet Institute in improving a non-commercial educational tool,
- and, when required (feedback form), on your consent, expressed notably via the checkboxes provided for this purpose.

---

### **6\. Data recipients**

Collected data is intended exclusively for:

- internal teams of the Learning Planet Institute involved in the WeLearn project.

No data is transmitted to third parties for commercial purposes.

---

### **7\. Security and hosting**

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are in place to ensure data security.

The infrastructure used is hosted within the European Union, mainly in France.

---

### **8\. Data retention**

Data from the feedback form is kept for a period proportionate to the purposes pursued, then deleted or anonymized.

Data processed temporarily (search, chat, uploaded documents) is not retained.

---

### **9\. Your rights**

In accordance with the provisions of the French Data Protection Act n°78-17 of January 6, 1978, as amended (“Loi Informatique et Libertés”), you have the following rights:

- right of access,
- right to rectification,
- right to erasure,
- right to restrict processing,
- right to object,
- right to data portability when applicable,
- right to lodge a complaint with the French Data Protection Authority (CNIL).

To exercise your rights, you can contact the DPO of the Learning Planet Institute at:  
**lamis.chamas@learningplanetinstitute.org**

---

### **10\. Policy updates**

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the platform or regulatory framework. The date of the last update will be indicated.
